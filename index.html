About Our Sunday School

Saint James Greek Orthodox Church Sunday School provides a loving, Christ-centered environment where children learn about their Orthodox faith, discover the teachings of Jesus Christ, and develop a personal relationship with God. Our program is designed to instill Orthodox Christian values and traditions while fostering spiritual growth and community.

Orthodox Christian Education

Age-appropriate lessons rooted in Scripture, Church tradition, and the teachings of the Holy Fathers, helping children understand their Orthodox heritage.

Divine Liturgy & Worship

Children learn to participate meaningfully in the Divine Liturgy and understand the beauty of Orthodox worship.

Faith Community

Building lasting friendships within our community in a safe, nurturing environment guided by dedicated teachers.

Our Mission

To guide children in developing a deep, personal relationship with Christ through the teachings and traditions of the Orthodox Church. We strive to create an environment where every child feels valued, loved, and supported in their spiritual journey.
